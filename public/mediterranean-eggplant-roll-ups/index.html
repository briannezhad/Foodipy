Mediterranean Eggplant Roll-Ups

  • By Foodipy
  • December 28, 2022

Ingredients:

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400F (200C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Slice the eggplant lengthwise into 1/4-inch slices. Brush both sides of the slices with olive oil and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake the eggplant slices for 10-15 minutes, until they are tender and starting to brown.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, feta cheese, parsley, mint, olives, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Once the eggplant slices are cool enough to handle, spread a spoonful of the quinoa mixture onto each slice. Roll up the slices tightly and place them seam-side down on the baking sheet.
  6. Bake the roll-ups for an additional 10-15 minutes, until they are heated through and the cheese is melted.
  7. Serve the roll-ups warm, garnished with additional chopped parsley and mint, if desired.

These eggplant roll-ups are a delicious and healthy appetizer or main dish, full of Mediterranean flavors and nutrients. The combination of eggplant, quinoa, feta cheese, and fresh herbs makes for a satisfying and flavorful dish that is sure to impress. Enjoy!
